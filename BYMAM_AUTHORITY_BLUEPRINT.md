## Authority Plan
1. **Pillar Content Foundation**
   - Publish 5 pillar articles with article + FAQ schema.
   - Include definitions and citations to support AI search indexing.
2. **Semantic Cluster Expansion**
   - Supporting articles reinforce pillars and create internal linking loops.
   - Each cluster page links to at least one pillar and service page.
3. **Flagship Research Asset**
   - The Modern Brand Infrastructure Report anchors backlink strategy and institutional credibility.
4. **Founder Authority**
   - Founder profile positions leadership, systems thinking, and executive credibility.
5. **Service Positioning**
   - Service pages emphasize engagement, partnership, and select clients.
6. **Entity & Ecosystem Signals**
   - Avoren Group references connect the studio to a broader organizational ecosystem.
