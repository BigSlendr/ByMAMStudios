FAQ

Quick answers to help you map the right level of engagement.

What’s included in “revision rounds”?

A revision round is a grouped set of feedback edits submitted at once. It keeps the project moving fast and organized.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Most builds can be split into 2–3 milestones depending on scope.

Will I be able to update my site myself?

Yes. If your build includes a CMS or editable sections, you’ll get a walkthrough after launch.

What if I’m not sure which package fits?

Book a call and we’ll map scope to the simplest package that meets your goals.
